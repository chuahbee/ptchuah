What’s the Differences between VEGO Window Films and Others?

High Infrared Reflection Characteristics

Normal Film

  • Small amount of heat is reflected.

  • With certain heat reflection effect but heat absorbing.
VEGO Window Film

  • Heat is greatly reflected.

  • High infrared reflective magnetron time limit (high temperature, high definition, low reflection)
Heat Absorbing Film

  • Heat enters car.

  • Poor insulation effect, harmful gas vitalization.
